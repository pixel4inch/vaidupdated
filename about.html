About Us

About VAID:

At VAID – Vasantha Architecture, Interiors & Landscape Designers, we believe every space should be functional, inspiring, and thoughtfully crafted. As one of the leading architects and interior designers in Hyderabad, our design approach blends modern, contemporary, and Vastu-aligned principles to create spaces refined with precision and purpose.

With a diverse portfolio covering architecture, interior design, landscape design, and project management, we’ve been shaping meaningful residential, commercial, and institutional spaces across multiple cities. At VAID, we take on projects that demand innovative, sustainable, and future-ready design solutions tailored to each client and site context.

We prioritize quality from the earliest design stages, ensuring clarity in decision-making, efficient detailing, and smooth execution. This upfront precision results in long-lasting, high-performance, and aesthetically superior buildings. Our proven quality-control processes, technical expertise, and years of industry experience empower clients with transparency, confidence, and exceptional results throughout the design and construction journey.
